Plot Introduction

This is a short film about a pair of slippers and the unnoticed, unspoken bond they share with their owner. In a world where life's fast pace often leaves little room for reflection, the slippers silently journey alongside the man, carrying memories of his past, his moments of solitude, and the fleeting connections he makes along the way.

The film is told without dialogue, allowing the audience to feel the emotional resonance through subtle movements, moments of pause, and visual storytelling. Each scene reveals the growing attachment between the man and his slippers, unnoticed by him but ever-present as a part of his journey through life.
